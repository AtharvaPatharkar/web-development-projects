## 🚀 How to Use

1️⃣ Open **`index.html`** in your browser.  
2️⃣ Enter the loan amount (₹), interest rate (%), and loan term (years).  
3️⃣ Click the **Calculate** button to get your monthly payment.  
4️⃣ Enjoy using your Loan Calculator!
